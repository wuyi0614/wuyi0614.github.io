This project line brings together work with climate investment and finance pilot programmes, the Asian Development Bank, China Resources Group, the State Grid, and city-level climate adaptation financing projects.

- Designs digital climate finance and carbon asset management systems.
- Studies green finance, voluntary carbon credits, and power-carbon data services.
- Develops policy and financing insights for regional and city-level climate transition.
