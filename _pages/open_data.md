## Open Data and Software

This page will collect datasets, software, and reproducible research resources connected to my work on climate finance, carbon markets, household emissions, ESG analytics, and computational sustainability.

Current resources to organize here include:

- **XCarbon V1.0**: simulation and prediction software for carbon markets.
- **Household carbon footprint data workflows**: reproducible pipelines for household emissions and lifestyle transition research.
- **ESG and sustainability knowledge resources**: benchmarks, knowledge graph resources, and LLM/NLP tools for sustainability standards and corporate evidence assessment.
- **City and infrastructure analytics**: research materials for climate finance, city-level emissions, railway station attractiveness, and AI infrastructure sustainability.
